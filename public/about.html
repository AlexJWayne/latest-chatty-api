About Latest Chatty

This is a community portal application for members of Shacknews.com, a website dedicated to video game news coverage. Shacknews.com has in-depth reviews, interviews, and breaking news on every major platform and developer. This application allows perusal of comment threads on the website's stories. Participation requires a valid account username and password.
